ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Andy! I'm 20 and I'm currently a senior student at Cornell University. I'm majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Game Design. I have been programming for over 6 years and have experience in making games for over 3 years.

Since I was a kid, I've always had three big goals on my bucket list: to make a positive impact on my community, to travel and learn about the diverse cultures of our planet, and to live a happy life.

While I might not be the best judge of how much impact I've made, I can confidently say that I'm living a happy life, thanks to the incredible people I've met and the perspectives I've gained from traveling the world. It may not seem like much, but I'm deeply grateful for everything I have.

When I'm not working on my next project, I love taking time off to be with friends and family. I also enjoy indulging in activities like tennis, skiing, cooking, watching movies, listening to music, and, of course, gaming.
